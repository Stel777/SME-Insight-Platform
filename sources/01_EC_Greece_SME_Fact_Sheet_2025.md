## 2. 2024 Performance — SME Value Added & Employment

> Growth rates split sharply by firm size. Micro firms drove aggregate gains; small and medium firms contracted.

### Overall SME sector (2024 actuals)
- Real value added: **+0.6%**
- Employment: **+2.9%**

### By size class (2024)

| Size class | Employees | Real VA growth | Employment growth |
|---|---|---|---|
| Micro | 0–9 | **+6.8%** | **+6.5%** |
| Small | 10–49 | **-4.1%** | **-1.6%** |
| Medium | 50–249 | Declined | Declined |

**Key insight:** Aggregate growth in 2024 is entirely micro-driven. Small and medium enterprises contracted in both value added and employment — highlighting a structural two-speed dynamic in Greek SMEs.

## 3. 2025 Projections

| Indicator | 2025 Forecast |
|---|---|
| SME real value added | **+7.0%** |
| SME employment | **+5.0%** |

- All size classes expected to see **value added** growth in 2025
- Only **micro firms** projected to see employment growth in 2025
- Small and medium enterprises remain under employment pressure
