## 2. Greece-Specific Data

### SME Lending
| Indicator | Value |
|---|---|
| SME lending flow change (period) | **+1.8 percentage points** |
| New SME lending in 2024 vs 2019 (pre-pandemic) | **Higher** — one of only ~12/21 European countries achieving this |
| Primary driver of lending recovery | Guarantee activity backed by EIF and EIB |

### Guarantee Activity (2024)
| Institution | Guarantee volume |
|---|---|
| European Investment Fund (EIF) | **€152 million** |
| European Investment Bank (EIB) | **€550 million** |
| Combined EIF + EIB | **€702 million** (2024) |

> Greece is specifically cited as a country where "new SME lending in 2024 was higher than in 2019" — a positive outlier in a European context of broadly stagnant SME loan stocks.

## 4. Access to Finance — Structural Issues for Greek SMEs

- Greece relies heavily on **public guarantee schemes** and EU-backed instruments — private equity and venture capital markets remain underdeveloped
- High interest rates relative to pre-pandemic create cost-of-capital pressure for micro-firms
- Non-performing loan legacy reduces banks' risk appetite for new SME exposure
- OECD highlights the absence of **robust evaluation mechanisms** to ensure guarantee-backed loans reach productive firms (vs. zombie firms)

---

## 5. Key Financing Pathways for Greek SMEs (from Scoreboard framework)

### Most common access routes (Greece)
1. Bank credit (dominant — relationship-based)
2. EIF/EIB-backed guarantee schemes
3. NextGenerationEU / RRF subsidised lending
4. ESPA (EU Structural Funds) grants — direct and intermediated
5. Leasing and asset-based finance (growing)
6. Equity / VC (nascent — underdeveloped)

## 6. What This Means for Consulting Engagement

| SME Behaviour | Implication for Engagement |
|---|---|
| Bank-driven lending triggers action | Bank requirement is a top engagement trigger (corroborated by interview data) |
| High cost of capital sensitivity | SMEs hyper-sensitive to advisory fees — justifies fixed-fee, staged approach |
| EU fund awareness gap | Many SMEs don't know they qualify — "Funding & Incentives" diagnostic is a credible entry offer |
| Guarantee reliance vs. equity | Growth-oriented SMEs need guidance on alternative finance — VC/equity conversations welcome |
